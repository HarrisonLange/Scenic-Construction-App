How to play

Line mixing is what a live A1 does on a musical: open each performer's channel (DCA) only while they speak or sing, so the mix stays clean and feedback-free.

  • Every character sits on a DCA bound to a key: Z X C V B N M.
  • Lines fall down their lane. Press & hold the key as the bar crosses the mix line, and release when the bar passes.
  • Holding the right DCA for the full line = Perfect. Opening the wrong DCA hurts your score.
  • You can also click/tap the DCA buttons at the bottom.
  • Practice runs one random scene with no pressure. Quiz runs 5 minutes from a random spot, scored like Guitar Hero — finish for a certificate.
