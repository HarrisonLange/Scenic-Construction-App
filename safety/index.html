🦺 Theatre Safety Training

This is your first-week shop certification — about 45 minutes of training a day, all week. You train on everything you're expected to know at your grade level: the core safety rules plus the specifics of every hand and power tool you've learned, this year and in past years. 100% is required on the quiz — in a real shop, "mostly safe" isn't safe. Miss a question and you'll retrain on that topic before trying again.

🧯 Day 5 — Certification Quiz

The quiz covers everything in your modules — every rule and every tool, from this year and every year before. Answer every question correctly to earn your safety certificate. Download it and submit it to your teacher.

What grade are you in?

Your training and quiz questions are matched to your grade level. You can change this any time from the badge in the top bar.
